LOVE AT FIRST PURR is a 501(c)(3) non-profit cat rescue organization dedicated to saving, rehabilitating, and rehoming cats in need across Staten Island, New York.

Found a Stray or Injured Cat?

Step-by-Step Rescue Instructions

If you’ve found a stray, injured, sick, or abandoned cat, follow these steps to provide safe, immediate care while you contact our rescue team:

  • Assess safety first: Approach slowly to avoid scaring the cat. Check for visible injuries, limping, or distress.
  • Contain safely: Use a carrier or cardboard box. Line with a towel and keep the cat warm, quiet, and away from children/pets.
  • Do NOT feed without guidance: Human food and improper diet can severely harm stray and injured cats.
  • Call us immediately: Our rescue team will guide you through drop-off or emergency pickup.

About LOVE AT FIRST PURR

Our Mission & Purpose

Our organization is dedicated to improving the lives of stray and abandoned cats through rescue, recovery, socialization, and adoption programs. We often receive calls from community members who have no other resources for assistance with stray and abandoned cats, and we step in to help where others cannot.

While we find permanent, loving homes for cats that are adoptable, we ensure that unadoptable cats are placed in safe, managed environments with ongoing care and support.

Through these efforts, we aim to promote animal welfare, reduce the suffering of homeless cats, and foster a compassionate community.
