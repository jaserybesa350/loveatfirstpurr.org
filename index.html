A second chance for every cat in Staten Island.

Our Mission

Our organization is dedicated to improving the lives of stray and abandoned cats through rescue, recovery, socialization, and adoption programs. We often receive calls from community members who have no other resources for assistance with stray and abandoned cats, and we step in to help where others cannot. While we find permanent, loving homes for cats that are adoptable, we ensure that unadoptable cats are placed in safe, managed environments with ongoing care and support. Through these efforts, we aim to promote animal welfare, reduce the suffering of homeless cats, and foster a compassionate community.

Rescue Guide & Adoption Policy

Our adoption process is designed to ensure the best match between our cats and their future families. We start with a pre-screening application, followed by an interview (virtual or in-person) to learn more about the adopter’s experience and home environment. Once an applicant is deemed suitable, we arrange virtual or live visits with the cat and conduct a home check to ensure a safe environment. References may be checked as needed. If approved, the adopter completes an adoption agreement, and we provide medical records and post-adoption support to ensure a smooth transition. We are committed to placing our cats in loving, responsible homes and remain a resource for adopters after the adoption is finalized. If for any reason an adopter can no longer keep their cat, we require that the cat be returned to our rescue.

Patient Stories

Every rescue is a journey of healing. Here are a few lives transformed by your support.

Successfully Adopted

The Miracle of Barnaby

Barnaby was discovered by a community member under a rusted vehicle, suffering from severe upper respiratory infection and extreme malnutrition. At just 6 weeks old, he had no other resources for help. Love At First Purr stepped in immediately.

After three weeks of intensive veterinary care, nebulizer treatments, and round-the-clock syringe feeding, Barnaby's spirit began to shine. Today, he is the king of a loving home in Staten Island, proving that no cat is too small to fight for.

"He went from a whisper of a kitten to a loud, purring companion."

In Managed Care

Finding Peace: Luna's Legacy

Not every cat is ready for a traditional home. Luna came to us with deep-seated trauma that made socialization nearly impossible. While many would have turned her away, our mission ensures that unadoptable cats are never abandoned.

Luna now lives in one of our safe, managed sanctuary environments. She receives ongoing medical support and the quiet space she needs to feel secure. She may never sleep on a human's bed, but she will never spend another night in fear or hunger.

Ensuring dignity and ongoing care for the "unadoptable."
